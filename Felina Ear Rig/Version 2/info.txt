Sakura's Felina Ear Rig V2:
    
    This rig was originally built to be used in Sakura's Animated Series 
    "The Lone Cyborg", but after that animation was scrapped it was repurposed for "the Lost Angel". The rig
    is very simple consisting of 5 bones, 1 Root, 4 Deforming.
    
    INSTRUCTIONS:
        1. Append this asset onto any rig, scale and position accordingly, 
        2. Parent Whole Armature to Main Head Bone using your method of choice
        
    Notes:
        There are controls under the Items > Properties (Press N to show), these will allow you to toggle the Antilag and Lattice Features of the rig
